3. **Run initial assessment** - Get your baseline compliance status
4. **Review gaps and remediate** - Address any compliance gaps identified
5. **Monitor ongoing compliance** - Set up monitoring and alerts

## Compliance Frameworks Supported

### Industry Standards
- **SOC 2** - Service Organization Control 2 for security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy
- **PCI DSS** - Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard for payment card security

### Privacy Regulations
- **GDPR** - General Data Protection Regulation (European Union)
- **CCPA** - California Consumer Privacy Act (United States)
- **LGPD** - Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (Brazil)
- **PIPEDA** - Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (Canada)
- **POPIA** - Protection of Personal Information Act (South Africa)
- **PDPA** - Personal Data Protection Act (Singapore)
- **APPI** - Act on the Protection of Personal Information (Japan)
